**Additional Notes:**
- **Power regulation**: The PYNQ‑Z1 accepts 5V (via USB) or 12V (via barrel jack). If using a 7.4V battery, a 12V boost regulator (or a 5V buck regulator for USB power) may be required. Consider adding a DC‑DC converter (e.g., item 14) to supply stable voltage.
- **Cables**: The B4B‑ZR to USB cable is custom; verify pinout matches the camera module’s datasheet. Alternatively, use the cable included with the camera (item 1) and omit item 3 if it already provides a standard USB connector.
- **Assembly**: 3D printed parts (items 4, 13) should be printed with appropriate infill and material for durability. STL files are to be designed based on the mechanical layout.
